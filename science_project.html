What's the best battery?

Problem

My family has used many batteries over the years, but I always wondered which was the best. I plan to test both name-brand and generic batteries, find out which battery lasts the longest.

Research

According to Wikipedia, the AA Alkaline battries are the most commonly used for consumers. I will only test these types of batteries.

Hypothesis

I beleive the most expensive battery will last the longest. But, I think one of the better generic batteries will be more cost effective.

Experiment

I will run a flashlight using different battery brands until the flashlight no longer generates any light.

Conclusion

Duracell was the longest lasting battery, but since generic CVS brand batteries were half the cost, they were greater value. I most things, I will only use CVS batteries from now on.
